About us


Race Pace Ricky is a simple Footrace predictor made by Eli Ahlander using Node.js, Axios, Express, BcryptJs and Sequelize. It is hosted using Heroku and a postgreSQL database.

The calculator is based on Peter Reigels T2=T1×(D2÷D1)^1.06 formula where T2 is the predicted time and T1 is the given time. Read more about Peter Reigel here: Peter Riegel Wikipedia
